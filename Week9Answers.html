What is the question you plan to answer?

What is the effect of transparency mechanisms on user's attitudes towards OBT, and to what extent is this effect mediated by the user's perceived control over their own personal data?

Why is this an important question?

Online Behavioral Targeting has revolutionized modern marketing, but it has also opened space for new threats and obstacles such as privacy concerns, polarization, and the exploitation of user’s vulnerabilities. Understanding the effect of transparency mechanisms on user’s attitudes is essential to shape the way forward in dealing with such new technologies.
